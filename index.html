BlindClock

The tournament clock that runs your poker night.

Blind levels, breaks, time bank, payouts — set it up in seconds, prop up the iPad, and just play. No account, no ads, no subscription.

Download on the App Store Version 1.9 · iOS 18.6+ · iPhone & iPad

Everything a host needs

Built for home games and club tournaments — accurate when the screen is off, readable from across the table.

♠

Precise tournament timer

Pause, resume, or skip levels. The clock stays accurate in the background and survives a force-quit — pick up right where you left off.

♥

Blind structure generator

Tell it your player count, chips, and target length — get a clean, casino-style structure with breaks in one tap.

♦

Payout calculator

Buy-ins, rebuys, and prize splits computed on the spot — settle up at the end of the night without a spreadsheet.

♣

Time bank

Give players extra decision time with configurable time banks, complete with warnings and sounds.

♠

Live Activity & Dynamic Island

The current level lives on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island. On a charging iPhone, StandBy turns it into a full-screen tournament clock.

♥

iPad table display

Rotate an iPad to landscape for a giant countdown with blinds on either side — the whole table reads it at a glance, and the screen stays awake.

♦

Template sharing

Export any tournament as a .blindclock file and AirDrop it to a friend — they import your exact structure in one tap.

♣

iCloud sync

Your tournament templates follow you across iPhone and iPad automatically — free for everyone.
